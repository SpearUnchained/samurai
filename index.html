ABOUT MY SAMURAI DOGS

My Samurai Dogs (MSD) is a virtual pet game that combines the power of Defi, NFT with a fully customizable gaming experience. Offering player a fully functional farming game and at the same time earn additional $DOGS tokens by holding and playing the game. Many in-game items and pets will be tradeable amongst players for $DOGS token on our marketplace, offering truly play2earn opportunities. My Samurai Dogs runs on the Binance Smart Chain due to its increasing popularity, as well as cheaper transaction and faster speed.

Features

COLLECT

BREED

SEASON AND EVENTS
Collect and maxout Legendary monster to claim lucrative prizes. Each season and many in-game events will be held to offer legendary items to the winners, the specific requirements varies, but it is based on a tiered system.
5
BATTLE
Unique battle modes will be available for all, participants in PVP and PVE will gain experience points and ultra rare rewards will be randomly awarded as well.
6
TRADING, RANKING AND SOCIAL FEATURES
Market place will be created to offer p2p exchanges for $DOGS and in-game items. Weekly/Monthly leaderboard will allow players to show off their highly trained Samurai Dogs to the world. Carefully cured in-game social functions will allow community building and more.
